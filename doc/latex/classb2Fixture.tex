\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
A fixture is used to attach a shape to a body for collision detection. A fixture inherits its transform from its parent. Fixtures hold additional non-\/geometric data such as friction, collision filters, etc. Fixtures are created via \mbox{\hyperlink{classb2Body_a40dda91b34418bb40e31e2db9b1b76a5}{b2\+Body\+::\+Create\+Fixture}}. \begin{DoxyWarning}{Warning}
you cannot reuse fixtures. 
\end{DoxyWarning}
